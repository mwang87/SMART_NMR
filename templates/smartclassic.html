Small Molecule Accurate Recognition Technology (SMART) is an Artificial Intelligence-based tool to generate structure predictions from NMR data (1H-13C-HSQC spectra). SMART Analysis compares your query HSQC spectrum against a library of > 50,000 Natural Products and provides you with SMILES, structures, cosine similarity, and molecular weight of your compound of interest.
