SMART 2.0 Drag and Drop


Small Molecule Accurate Recognition Technology (SMART) is an Artificial Intelligence-based tool to generate structure hypotheses from NMR data (1H-13C-HSQC spectra). SMART Analysis compares your query HSQC spectrum against a library of > 50,000 Natural Products and provides you with SMILES, structures, cosine similarity, and molecular weight of your compound of interest.

Users from Europe: please change your region settings in order to obtain COMMA-separated (.csv) instead of semicolon-separated files. An alternative way is to use Peak Entry on this Webpage.
